About Me

Hello, I'm Sophie and I did a bachelors degree in media technology in St. Pölten (Austria) and studied user experience design for one semester in The Hague (The Netherlands). Next to studying, I worked as a technical assistant at the Campus und City Radio 94.4 in St.Pölten.

I want to work in web development, at a radio station or as user experience designer. As I have gained skills in each of these fields, I can develop my skills further in each of this directions. In concrete, I have skills in HTML5, CSS3, PHP, Javascript, Java, Adobe Creative Cloud and skills at a radio station + UX design .

My inspiration comes from my daily life and other people. Design is About being exact for me and I care a lot about details. I am punctual and always manage to organize my work well which I learned from working next to studying. I am a team player but also enjoy to work alone sometimes - the mixture works for me!
